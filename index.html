Accessing WordPress

Now, here is where it can get slightly confusing. Stay with me now.

  • Click on your WordPress Instance
  • To find what your username and password is, you will want to click connect using SSH for your password, and your username will be below (called Bitnami) along with your public IP.
  • Once you open the large black screen that says bitnami with all the code on the page, you will want to type in this exact code to retrieve your password. cat bitnami_application_password

    • https://www.how2shout.com/how-to/how-to-install-wordpress-on-amazon-lightsail-aws.html
  • Your password will now pop up on the bitnami page, copy it, put it somewhere in your notes or in a word/pages doc. Just make sure to keep it safe.

    • https://www.how2shout.com/how-to/how-to-install-wordpress-on-amazon-lightsail-aws.html

Using WordPress

Now, the fun part! You are finally able to use your wordpress account!

  • Now, before logining in, lets make sure it works. Type your public IP into the search bar. When the page reloads, there should be text that says Hello World!
  • To get to your login page, you will type your public IP address and then /wp-admin after it.
  • Type in your bitnami username in the username box, type your super secret password in the password box

    • https://www.how2shout.com/how-to/how-to-install-wordpress-on-amazon-lightsail-aws.html

And BOOM just like that you are in and able to create any types of blogs your heart desires.
